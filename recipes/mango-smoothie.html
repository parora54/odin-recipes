Mango Smoothie Recipe


Description

Ultra creamy mango smoothie is the best summertime smoothie. It's made with mango and just a handful of ingredients.



Ingredients

  • Frozen Mango Chunks
  • Greek Yogurt
  • Milk
  • Honey (to taste)


Steps:

  1. In a blender, put in a generous amount of frozen mango
  2. Add in the greek yogurt and blend together until smooth
  3. Add in a little bit of milk and any honey, if you like
